Child Health Care

Prenatal Care: Monitoring Mother and Baby

Regular prenatal checkups are essential for a healthy pregnancy. These visits help monitor the health of both the mother and the developing baby.

  • Schedule regular appointments with your healthcare provider.
  • Undergo recommended screenings and tests.
  • Discuss any concerns or symptoms with your doctor.

Nutrition During Pregnancy: Fueling Healthy Growth

A balanced and nutritious diet is vital during pregnancy to support the baby's growth and the mother's health.

  • Consume a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins.
  • Ensure adequate intake of folic acid, iron, calcium, and other essential nutrients.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Limit caffeine, alcohol, and processed foods.
